## Overview

Shelf is a deliberately quiet reading tracker. It answers one question — *what am I reading, and what did I think?* — and refuses to gamify the rest.

## The problem

Most reading apps turn books into a leaderboard. Streaks, yearly goals, friends' activity. I wanted the opposite: a calm place to keep a shelf.

## What I built

A SwiftUI app backed by CloudKit so your shelf follows you across devices with no separate login. Add a book, set a status, jot a few lines. That's the whole app.

## Outcome

Shipping something *small on purpose* was harder than it sounds — every cut feature was a temptation. The restraint is the product.
