## Overview

Snob is a logbook for the obsessive home barista: beans, grind, dose, ratio, time — and whether the cup was actually any good.

## What I built

A Next.js app with a Postgres backend and a brew timer that records the variables as you go, then surfaces trends so you can chase the perfect extraction.

## Outcome

Turns out *measuring* the ritual changed it. I got pickier, and the coffee got better.
