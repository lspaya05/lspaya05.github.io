## Overview

Glyph is a browser playground for variable fonts — drag the axes, watch the letterforms breathe, and pull out the exact instance you want.

## What I built

A canvas-based specimen tool that exposes every registered axis as a live slider and renders custom sample text at any weight, width, or optical size.

## Outcome

Variable fonts are a *design space*, not a file. Making that space tangible changed how I pick type.
