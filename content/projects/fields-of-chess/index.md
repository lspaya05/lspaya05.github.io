## Overview

Feed it a PGN — the move list of a single chess game — and it draws a one-of-a-kind poster from the paths the pieces traced across the board.

## What I built

A p5.js sketch that parses PGN, maps each piece's journey to a layered field of strokes, and exports print-resolution art. Famous games make surprisingly beautiful prints.

## Outcome

A reminder that *constraints* are generative fuel. The 64 squares did most of the design work.
