## Overview

Point your camera at a receipt; Billbreak reads the line items, lets everyone tap what they had, and splits tax and tip *proportionally* — no mental math at the table.

## What I built

On-device OCR with Apple's Vision framework, a fuzzy line-item parser, and a fast tap-to-claim UI. Nothing leaves the phone.

## Outcome

The OCR was the easy part; the hard part was the *social* UX of who-had-what without it feeling like a spreadsheet.
