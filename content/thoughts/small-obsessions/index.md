---
title: Small obsessions are underrated
tag: Essay
date: "Mar 2026"
order: 2
excerpt: The case for going embarrassingly deep on something that doesn't matter to anyone else.
---
There's a particular joy in caring far too much about something that matters to no one. The right grind size. The exact shade of a link color. A keyboard layout you'll spend more time configuring than using.

These obsessions look like waste from the outside. From the inside they're how you learn to *see* — to notice the difference between almost-right and right, which turns out to transfer everywhere.
