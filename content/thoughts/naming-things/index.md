---
title: Naming things is the whole job
tag: Random ideas
date: "Dec 2025"
order: 6
excerpt: Every hard problem I've had this year was secretly a naming problem.
---
When code resists me, it's almost never the logic. It's that I've named something wrong, and the wrong name is quietly lying to me about what it does.

Rename it honestly and the bug becomes obvious. Naming isn't decoration on top of thinking — it *is* the thinking, made visible.
