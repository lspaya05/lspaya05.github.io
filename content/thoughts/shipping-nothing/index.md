I finished almost nothing last year. A dozen repos, three half-apps, one abandoned hardware build. By the usual scoreboard, a wash.

But each unfinished thing taught one concrete skill that outlived it. The abandoned synth taught me oscillators. The dead app taught me CRDTs. **Shipping** isn't the only way to learn — sometimes the scaffolding is the lesson.
